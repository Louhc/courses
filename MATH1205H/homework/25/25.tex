        If $L$ is not the zero function,
        $$
            \exists v\in V:L(v)\neq 0
        $$
        So
        $$
            \forall x\in \mathbb{R}:x=\frac{x}{L(v)}L(v)=L(\frac{x}{L(v)}v)
        $$
        Therefore, $L$ is surjective.
